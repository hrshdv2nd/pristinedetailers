# Pristine Detailers — Product Marketing Context

## Business Overview
**Pristine Detailers** is a premium mobile car detailing company based in **Melbourne, Victoria, Australia**. We come to the customer — no workshop, no waiting room. Same tools, same certified technicians, same results as a fixed studio.

## Core Services
- **Ceramic Coating** — professional-grade, 3–8 year protection, UV resistance, hydrophobic finish
- **Paint Protection Film (PPF)** — full-body or partial wrap, stone chip and scratch protection
- **Paint Correction** — machine polish to remove swirls, scratches, and oxidation from the clear coat
- **Interior Detailing** — deep clean, leather conditioning, odour elimination
- **Full Detailing** — comprehensive interior + exterior packages
- **Maintenance Washes** — ongoing care for clients with coatings or PPF

## Membership Program
Monthly membership gives customers:
- Discounted services (priority pricing on all bookings)
- Priority scheduling
- Monthly maintenance included
- Exclusive member rates on ceramic and PPF upgrades
The membership is positioned as a "car care subscription" — set-and-forget protection.

## Target Audience (ICP)
**Primary:** Melbourne car owners who care about their vehicle's appearance and resale value.
- Age 28–55, professional, owns a car worth $30K+
- Drives a European, Japanese performance, or luxury vehicle (BMW, Mercedes, Audi, Toyota Landcruiser, Porsche, Tesla)
- Lives or works in: Toorak, South Yarra, Brighton, St Kilda, Richmond, Hawthorn, Bayside suburbs, Mornington Peninsula
- Values quality over price — will pay premium for expertise
- Time-poor: appreciates the mobile, come-to-you model
- Concerned about stone chips on freeways, bird droppings, car park scratches

**Secondary:** Car enthusiasts and collectors (track cars, weekend drivers, show cars)

## Brand Voice
- **Confident, expert, never salesy** — we talk like technicians who know their craft
- **Clear and direct** — no fluff, no buzzwords
- **Melbourne-specific** — reference local roads, suburbs, weather, lifestyle
- **Premium but approachable** — not pretentious, just excellent
- Tone: like a trusted mechanic friend who happens to be an expert

## Key Differentiators
1. **Mobile** — we come to your home, office, or car park
2. **Certified technicians** — not weekend detailers; trained professionals
3. **Membership model** — ongoing relationship, not one-off transactions
4. **Melbourne expertise** — we know what this city does to paint (bird droppings in Toorak, gravel roads on the Mornington Peninsula, UV on the bay)

## Competitors
- Fixed-location detailing studios (IDA-certified, but you have to drive there)
- Generic mobile wash services (lower quality, no coatings)
- Dealership detailing packages (overpriced, generic)

## Tone Examples
- Good: "Stone chips on the freeway aren't bad luck — they're physics. PPF stops them before they reach your paint."
- Good: "A ceramic coating isn't a magic shield. It's a chemical bond with your clear coat that repels water, UV, and most contaminants for years."
- Avoid: "We offer the best service in Melbourne!" or "Our team is passionate about cars!"
