## Campaign Context

The Membership program is positioned as a "car care subscription" — set-and-forget protection for Melbourne car owners who value their vehicle but don't want to think about scheduling. Target intent: someone searching for ongoing or recurring car care, a plan, or a subscription. They own a $30K+ car, they're time-poor, and they live in postcode areas where pride of ownership is high.

---

## Negative Keywords (3)

```
-cheap
-DIY
-car wash
```

**Rationale:**
- `-cheap`: Filters price-sensitive searchers who will churn or never convert at premium price points
- `-DIY`: Removes hobbyist/self-detailer intent — wrong customer for a done-for-you service
- `-car wash`: Captures generic wash queries that aren't aligned with coating/correction/membership services

- **Device:** Desktop and mobile even split — booking likely happens on mobile, research on desktop
- **Schedule:** Run Mon–Sat, peak hours 7–9am and 6–9pm (commuter/evening search windows)
- **Bid strategy:** Target CPA — start with manual CPC to gather data if conversion tracking is new; switch to tCPA after 30+ conversions
- **Audience layers (Observation):** In-market for auto services, luxury auto owners — observe performance without restricting reach
