---

## Campaign Context

The Membership ad set targets Melbourne car owners who don't know they need a subscription yet — Meta creates demand before someone searches. The angle here is identity and convenience: your car should get consistent, expert care without you having to think about it. We're selling the feeling of a car that's always ready, not the mechanics of monthly bookings.

Visual recommendation for all variants: before/after split showing dull paint vs. gloss finish on a European or luxury SUV parked in an inner-Melbourne street. Natural light. No studio. No props.

---

## Ad Variants

### Variant 1 — Angle: Pain Point (Neglect)

**Primary Text (120 chars):**
> Your BMW needs care every month. Most owners remember twice a year. Pristine Members never miss a beat — we come to you.

**Headline (25 chars):**
> Never Miss a Detail Again

**Description (26 chars):**
> Monthly. Mobile. Pristine.

**Placement Notes:**
- Strong for Facebook Feed — the opening line stops the scroll on car-owner audiences
- Works as a Story with first sentence as text overlay on a before/after visual

---

### Variant 2 — Angle: Identity (Your suburb, your standard)

**Primary Text (97 chars):**
> Toorak to Brighton, your car gets the same expert care every month. Certified techs, at your door.

**Headline (26 chars):**
> Your Suburb, Your Schedule

**Description (26 chars):**
> We come to you, every time

**Placement Notes:**
- Geography-first hook resonates on Instagram Reels and Stories where local feel matters
- Pair with a visual showing a Pristine technician working in a recognisable Bayside driveway or underground car park
- Test narrow-targeting to the listed postcodes first; expand if CPM becomes unfavourable

---

### Variant 3 — Angle: Simplicity / Value Clarity

**Primary Text (122 chars):**
> Set-and-forget car care. One plan, monthly maintenance, priority booking, member rates on ceramic and PPF. Cancel anytime.

**Headline (23 chars):**
> Melbourne Car Care Plan

**Description (26 chars):**
> Priority. Mobile. Members.

**Placement Notes:**
- Benefit-stacking format — works best in Facebook Feed where users pause to read
- "Cancel anytime" handles the commitment objection directly in the copy
- Pair with a clean graphic listing the 4 membership benefits in a visual format

## Targeting

### Core Audience

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Location | Melbourne metro — Toorak (3142), South Yarra (3141), Brighton (3186), Hawthorn (3122), Richmond (3121), Malvern (3145), St Kilda East (3183), Bayside LGA |
| Age | 28–55 |
| Gender | All |
| Interests | BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Audi, Porsche, Tesla, luxury vehicles, car detailing, vehicle maintenance, premium lifestyle |
| Behaviours | Engaged shoppers, frequent international travellers (proxy for affluent), homeowners |

### Lookalike Audience

- **Seed:** Upload existing Pristine member email list (or /membership page converters)
- **LAL size:** 1–3% for prospecting; 3–5% if volume is too low
- **Exclude:** Current members (suppress by email list) and anyone who converted in the last 14 days

### Interest Stacks to A/B Test

- Stack A: Luxury auto brands (BMW + Audi + Mercedes + Porsche)
- Stack B: Broad car maintenance + premium lifestyle
- Stack C: LAL only (cleanest signal, test separately)

## Retargeting — /booking Non-Converters

**Audience:** Visited /booking but did not complete a booking (7-day window)
**Frequency cap:** 4x per week
**Window:** 7–14 days post-visit

**Ad Copy:**

> Primary text (103 chars):
> You were this close. Your Pristine membership is still here — monthly care, we come to you. Join today.

> Headline (22 chars):
> Still Thinking It Over?

> Description (27 chars):
> Your spot's waiting. Book now.

**Notes:**
- This audience has already signalled high intent — shorter copy, direct CTA
- If budget allows, run a separate retargeting creative offering a "first month" incentive (check with business on promotion approval before launching)

## Budget Recommendation

- **Testing phase (Weeks 1–2):** Split evenly across 3 variants and 3 interest stacks
- **Scaling:** After 500+ impressions per variant, pause lowest CTR; double down on top 2
- **Retargeting budget:** 15–20% of total campaign budget (warm audience, cheaper CPM)
