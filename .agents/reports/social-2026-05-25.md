Ceramic coating isn't a force field. Here's exactly what it does — and why Melbourne conditions make it worth understanding.

A ceramic coating is a liquid polymer that chemically bonds to your clear coat at a molecular level — it's not a film, not a wax, and it doesn't peel off after six months.
