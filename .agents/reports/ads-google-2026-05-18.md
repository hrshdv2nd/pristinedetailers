## Melbourne Seasonal Context — May Brief for Creative Team

1. **Deciduous trees dropping leaves** — Toorak's tree-lined streets (Orrong Rd, St Georges Rd, Kooyong Rd) are in peak leaf and sap season; bird activity spikes; ceramic's chemical resistance to organic acid etching is the most urgent sale right now
2. **Autumn rain returns** — Melbourne averages higher rainfall May–August; the hydrophobic water-sheeting demo is visually compelling and seasonally relevant; ideal hook for video creative or Meta (if Meta brief runs this week)
3. **Pre-winter protection window** — Many ICP buyers think about paint protection in spring and autumn; the "protect before winter" framing creates urgency without manufactured scarcity
4. **Freeway grit season** — Road salt and sand used on Mornington Peninsula roads in winter; PPF is the harder sell here, but ceramic coating's contamination resistance is a relevant secondary message
