The most common misconception about ceramic coating is that it protects against scratches. It doesn't — at least not in the way most people expect. What it does is chemically bond to your clear coat, creating a hydrophobic and UV-resistant surface that lasts 3–8 years depending on the grade applied.

UV fade — which quietly kills the depth and gloss of any unprotected paint — slows significantly. Maintenance washes take less time. The paint underneath stays closer to original condition for years longer than it otherwise would.
