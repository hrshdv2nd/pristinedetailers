## Victoria's Legal Window Tint Limits

Victoria's Road Safety (Vehicles) Regulations set Visible Light Transmission (VLT) minimums — the percentage of light a window must allow through. Lower VLT means darker tint. Here's what's legal:

| Window | Minimum VLT | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Windscreen (forward of driver) | 70% VLT | Tint strip permitted at the top |
| Front side windows (driver + passenger) | 35% VLT | Cannot go darker than this |
| Rear side windows | 20% VLT | Darker tint permitted behind the driver's row |
| Rear windscreen | 20% VLT | Only if vehicle has external rear-view mirrors |

One thing most people miss: factory-tinted glass counts toward the total. If your rear glass comes from the factory at 30% VLT and you add a 50% film, the combined result is 15% — below the legal minimum. Before we recommend a film grade, we measure your existing glass. No guesswork, no RMS fines.

## How Mobile Window Tinting Works

The mobile setup brings the same professional-grade films and tools used in fixed studios directly to you. Here's the process:

1. **Assessment** — We measure your existing glass VLT and confirm what's legal for each window position on your vehicle.
2. **Surface preparation** — Windows are cleaned and decontaminated. Old adhesive, residue, or existing tint is removed before any film goes down.
3. **Film cutting** — Film is cut to your exact window dimensions on-site using pre-cut templates matched to your vehicle make and model.
4. **Application** — Film is installed wet, squeegeed flat, and trimmed to fit. No bubbles, no lifting edges.
5. **Curing** — Film takes 3–5 days to fully cure (longer in Melbourne winters). Avoid winding your windows down during this period.
6. **Final inspection** — We check every edge and corner before we leave. You see the finished result before we pack up.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How long does window tinting take?**  
Most sedans take 2–3 hours. SUVs and larger vehicles run 3–4 hours. We confirm an exact time estimate at booking based on your vehicle make and model.

**Can you tint in an underground car park?**  
Yes — provided there's adequate lighting and enough clearance to work around the vehicle. Most apartment car parks in South Yarra, Richmond, Hawthorn, and Toorak qualify. We confirm suitability before booking.

**Does window tinting void my warranty?**  
No. Window tinting is a cosmetic modification and does not affect your vehicle's mechanical or structural warranty. We use certified films that don't interfere with defogging elements or internal electronics.

**What VLT is legal in Victoria for front windows?**  
Front side windows — driver and passenger — must allow a minimum of 35% VLT. Our standard front film sits exactly at 35% for full compliance. We don't recommend going darker on front glass regardless of what other installers offer.
