## Platform: LinkedIn

**Caption:**

Winter in Melbourne is the season most car owners ignore — and the season that does the most cumulative damage to paint.

Here's why: cold and wet conditions mean cars get washed less often. Bird droppings — highly acidic — sit on unprotected paintwork longer. Road grime from Melbourne's inner and eastern arterials layers up across multiple wet weeks. Water spots from rain on an uncoated surface etch slowly but persistently into the clear coat. By the time spring arrives, the damage is already done.

For business owners and fleet managers, this compounds quickly. A fleet of service vehicles or company cars left unmaintained through winter doesn't just look unprofessional — it accelerates paint degradation that hits resale and trade-in value at the end of the lease cycle.

A winter maintenance detail — decontamination, wash, and sealant top-up — takes less than two hours, comes to your location, and keeps paintwork protected until the weather improves. Pristine Detailers operates across Melbourne's Inner East, Bayside, and CBD-adjacent suburbs. If you're managing vehicles for your business, get in touch about our corporate membership — built for ongoing care without the scheduling overhead.
