## Campaign Notes

**Bid strategy:** Target CPA (set after 50+ conversions; start with Maximise Conversions to gather data)
**Budget signal:** Prioritise Toorak, Brighton, and Hawthorn postcodes via location bid adjustments (+20%)
**Ad schedule:** Skew towards Thursday–Sunday (weekend bookings peak; car owners thinking about their vehicle)
**Device:** Desktop and mobile parity — these buyers research on both
**Match type discipline:** Run exact match only for the first 30 days; open to broad match modifier once search term report is clean
