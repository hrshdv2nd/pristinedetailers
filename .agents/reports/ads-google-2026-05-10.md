---

## Negative Keywords (3)

```
-self service car wash
-car detailing course
-car detailing products
```

**Rationale:**
- `-self service car wash`: Filters DIY-wash seekers at a more specific phrase level than the broad `-car wash` already in use
- `-car detailing course`: Removes training and certification seekers — irrelevant to a service purchase
- `-car detailing products`: Blocks people buying supplies to do it themselves (wrong funnel entirely)
