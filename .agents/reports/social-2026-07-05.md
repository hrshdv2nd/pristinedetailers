## Platform: Facebook

**Caption:**
Ceramic coating confuses a lot of people — they assume it's a premium wax or something that makes the car look glossy. It's neither.

Ceramic coating is a nano-ceramic compound that chemically bonds to your clear coat. Once cured, it forms a permanent barrier that contaminants — bird droppings, brake dust, tree sap — can't penetrate or etch through. It doesn't wash off or fade the way wax and sealants do. A quality coating carries a manufacturer warranty of up to 8 years.

If you drive a car worth protecting — a BMW, Range Rover, Tesla, or anything above $30K — ceramic coating is the most cost-effective way to protect its paint and resale value long-term. We come to you across Melbourne's Inner East and Bayside suburbs. No workshop, no waiting room.
