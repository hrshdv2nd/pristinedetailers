# Google Search Ads — Ceramic Coating Focus (Iteration 2)
**Date:** 2026-05-20 (Wednesday — Week 21)
**Week:** 21 | **Service Focus:** Ceramic Coating (Week 5 rotation)
**Platform:** Google Search Ads (Responsive Search Ad)
**Campaign:** GOOG_Lead_Search_CeramicCoating_Melbourne_2026W21
**Landing Page:** /booking
**Prior set:** See ads-google-2026-05-18.md — this is a fresh creative iteration for A/B testing alongside the May 18 headlines

**Context:** The May 18 set anchored on longevity, geo-pinning, and Melbourne's environmental threats (hydrophobic, UV, bird droppings). This iteration tests three new angles: seasonal urgency (protect before winter), authority/trust differentiation (certified vs. weekend detailers), and objection removal (no drop-off required). New keywords target South Yarra and Hawthorn geo-intent, which the May 18 set underweighted. Negative keyword additions close gaps that may have let DIY-adjacent and informational queries through.

**RSA Pinning & Combination Notes:**
- **H1** — rotate into Position 2 or 3 alongside the May 18 anchor headline "Ceramic Coating Melbourne" (H1, pinned); "Protect Before Winter Sets In" is the urgency signal that follows the keyword match and drives clicks from research-phase buyers who are timing their purchase
- **H2** — strongest in Position 3; works alongside either H1 or the May 18 "Hydrophobic. UV. Bird Drop." headline; do not pin both simultaneously — pick one Melbourne-threat headline per impression to avoid redundancy
- **H3** — high-differentiation headline for searchers using terms like "ceramic coating near me" or "mobile ceramic coating Melbourne"; positions Pristine against the DIY kit market and the weekend-detailer market in a single short phrase; test unpinned first to see which position it wins
- **H4** — geo-specific headline for the South Yarra and Hawthorn ad groups; replace "South Yarra" with "Toorak & Hawthorn" or "Brighton & Hampton" for the Bayside ad group; personalisation at the headline level lifts CTR by approximately 1–2 percentage points vs. generic Melbourne
- **H5** — removes the #1 objection to mobile services (will they actually come to me?) by naming the customer's driveway as the service location; strongest alongside H1 or H4 for geo-targeted impressions

## Ad Descriptions (90 chars max each)

| # | Description | Char Count |
|---|-------------|-----------|
| 1 | Bonds to your clear coat. Rain, bird drops, UV repelled for years. Mobile to your door. | 87 |
| 2 | Certified techs, not weekend detailers. Applied at your home or office. Book online. | 84 |

**Notes:**
- **D1** opens with the technical mechanism ("bonds to your clear coat") then immediately lists the three Melbourne-specific threats the coating defeats; "Mobile to your door" closes with the differentiator — the sequence is mechanism → benefit → USP
- **D2** is the trust description: the phrase "not weekend detailers" explicitly addresses the most common scepticism about mobile detailing services (that they're amateurs); "Applied at your home or office" restates the mobile USP without repeating "mobile" — avoids word fatigue with D1
- Rotate D1 and D2 — do not pin either; Google's RSA optimiser will determine which combination of headline + description drives the highest CTR and conversion rate
- Neither description names a price; ceramic coating pricing requires a quote conversation; adding a price point without context risks disqualifying high-LTV buyers who would happily pay but baulk at an out-of-context number

## Exact-Match Keywords (5 — new additions to complement May 18 set)

```
[ceramic coating south yarra]
[mobile ceramic coating near me]
[ceramic coating hawthorn]
[car ceramic coating cost melbourne]
[mobile car detailing ceramic coating]
```

**Rationale:**
- `[ceramic coating south yarra]` — South Yarra (3141) sits between Toorak and Prahran; high-density of European cars, renters and owners with premium vehicles who want mobile (no driveway at home, but building car parks qualify); volume is lower than [ceramic coating toorak] but CPC is also lower and conversion quality is comparable
- `[mobile ceramic coating near me]` — captures high-intent searches with the explicit "near me" modifier; these searchers have already decided they want mobile application, not a studio; pairs with H5 ("Free Quote at Your Driveway") to directly answer the search intent
- `[ceramic coating hawthorn]` — Hawthorn (3122) is an underserved geo-intent term; the suburb has the same ICP characteristics as Toorak (older homes, luxury cars, professionals) but lower advertiser competition; this term likely has a lower CPC for equivalent quality traffic
- `[car ceramic coating cost melbourne]` — cost intent queries are high-intent; this searcher is in the decision phase, comparing options; serve them an ad that answers the intent (mobile, professional, no DIY) without naming a price, then let /booking handle the quote conversion
- `[mobile car detailing ceramic coating]` — bridges the detailing search intent with ceramic coating; captures buyers researching full-service mobile detailing who are open to upgrading to ceramic once they understand what it does; upsell opportunity from a "detailing" search

**Note on overlap with May 18 keywords:** The May 18 set runs [ceramic coating melbourne], [mobile ceramic coating melbourne], [ceramic coating toorak], [car paint protection melbourne], [ceramic coating bayside melbourne]. The five above are additive — run in the same campaign or a parallel ad group; do not replace the May 18 terms.

## Negative Keywords (3 — new additions)

```
-ceramic coating training
-how to apply ceramic coating
-ceramic spray wax
```

**Rationale:**
- `-ceramic coating training` — filters detailing trade students and operators researching accreditation; they are suppliers, not service buyers; adds to the existing `-ceramic coating course` negative from May 18
- `-how to apply ceramic coating` — informational intent query; this searcher wants a tutorial, not a booking; they land on /journal content, not /booking; if the journal has a strong organic presence for this term, the paid impression is wasted spend
- `-ceramic spray wax` — the cheapest category of ceramic-branded products (Mothers, Chemical Guys, Meguiar's consumer sprays); a searcher using this term is shopping for a $30 product, not a $1,000+ professional application; excluding prevents low-intent clicks and protects Quality Score

## Targeting Additions (Iteration 2)

### New Ad Group: Hawthorn / Kew / Camberwell

Add a third ad group targeting the inner-east pocket that sits north of the May 18 Inner East group:

**Postcodes:** Hawthorn (3122), Kew (3101), Camberwell (3124), Balwyn (3103), Box Hill South (3128)

**Headlines to swap:** Replace H4 "South Yarra to Brighton" with "Hawthorn to Camberwell" for this ad group.

**Why:** This postcode cluster has comparable household income and vehicle profile to Toorak but is slightly farther from the CBD — these buyers are more likely to search with suburb modifiers because they're less confident mobile services will reach them. The geo-specific headline + exact-match keyword combination ([ceramic coating hawthorn]) directly answers that concern.

### Device & Schedule Notes

- Consistent with May 18 set: run Mon–Sat, suppress Sunday
- Add a +10% bid adjustment for searches occurring on weekday evenings (6–9pm) — autumn evenings prompt "what happened to my car today" moments after noticing bird droppings or rain spots
- **New adjustment:** +5% for Saturday mornings (9am–12pm) — this is when car owners are most likely to be home, in the driveway, and booking home-visit services
