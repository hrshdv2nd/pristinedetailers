## Keyword Recommendations

### Exact Match — 5 Keywords

```
[ceramic coating melbourne]
[mobile ceramic coating melbourne]
[ceramic coating toorak]
[ceramic coating brighton melbourne]
[car ceramic coating bayside]
```

**Rationale:**
- `[ceramic coating melbourne]` — highest volume, broad catch-all for the city
- `[mobile ceramic coating melbourne]` — captures the core differentiator in the search; strong buying intent
- `[ceramic coating toorak]` — suburb-level targeting, premium postcode, lower CPC, higher conversion rate expected
- `[ceramic coating brighton melbourne]` — Bayside's anchor suburb, strong Beemer/Merc ownership density
- `[car ceramic coating bayside]` — broader Bayside net, captures Port Phillip salt-air concern searchers

---

### Negative Keywords — 3 to Exclude

```
[diy ceramic coating]
[ceramic coating kit]
[cheap ceramic coating]
```

**Rationale:**
- `[diy ceramic coating]` — researching, not buying; will inflate CPC and lower conversion rate
- `[ceramic coating kit]` — product shoppers, not service buyers
- `[cheap ceramic coating]` — price-anchors the wrong way; Pristine's audience pays for quality, not the lowest quote
