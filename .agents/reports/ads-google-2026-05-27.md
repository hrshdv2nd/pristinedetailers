## Targeting Recommendations

- **Geography:** Melbourne metro — tighten radius to Inner East, Bayside, and CBD. Include Mornington Peninsula as a secondary zone.
- **Bid adjustments:** +20% for Toorak (3142), Brighton (3186), South Yarra (3141), Hawthorn (3122) postcodes
- **Device:** Mobile-preferred bidding (+15% mobile) — service searchers often check their phone at the car park or after a drive
- **Ad schedule:** Run 6am–9pm AEST; suppress midnight–5am (minimal intent, wasted spend)
- **Audience layering (observation):** Add "luxury car owners," "auto enthusiasts," and "in-market: auto detailing" as audience signals without restricting reach

---

## Budget Recommendation

- **Daily budget:** $40–60 AUD (testing phase)
- **Bid strategy:** Maximise conversions (once 30+ conversions recorded); manual CPC to start
- **Target CPA:** $80–120 AUD per `/booking` form submit
- **Learning period:** Allow 14 days before optimising — RSA needs impression volume to learn combinations
