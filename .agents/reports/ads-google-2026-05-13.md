## Negative Keywords (3)

```
-diy paint correction
-touch up paint
-paint correction course
```

**Rationale:**
- `-diy paint correction`: Blocks hobbyists researching how to do it themselves — wrong funnel, will never convert to a booked service
- `-touch up paint`: Filters searchers buying touch-up pens or kits for rock chips; completely different product intent
- `-paint correction course`: Removes detailing students and professionals looking for training — irrelevant to a service booking

## Paint Correction-Specific Creative Notes

Melbourne context to brief creative team on:

1. **Bird droppings in Toorak** — gum trees on Orrong Rd and St Georges Rd are notorious; acid etching from droppings is a real purchase trigger for this suburb
2. **Freeway swirl accumulation** — Monash, Eastern, and South Eastern Freeways throw fine gravel and grit; automated car washes then grind it into the clear coat
3. **Apartment car park scratches** — High-density South Yarra and Richmond buildings have tight multi-deck car parks where door dings and pillar scuffs are constant
4. **Pre-coating prep** — Customers considering ceramic coating after correction are high LTV; consider a follow-up remarketing message targeting paint correction bookers with a ceramic coating upgrade offer 7–14 days post-service
