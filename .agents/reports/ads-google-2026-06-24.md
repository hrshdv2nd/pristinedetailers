## Responsive Search Ad — PPF Melbourne

### Headlines (30 chars max)

| # | Headline | Chars |
|---|----------|-------|
| H1 | PPF Melbourne \| We Come To You | 30 |
| H2 | Stop Stone Chips On Your Drive | 30 |
| H3 | Paint Protection Film \| Mobile | 30 |
| H4 | Full-Body PPF At Your Driveway | 30 |
| H5 | Certified PPF Tech. Book Now | 28 |

**Pin guidance:**
- Pin H1 ("PPF Melbourne | We Come To You") to Position 1 — intent match + mobile differentiator in one
- H3 and H4 rotate in Position 2 — "Paint Protection Film" spells it out for searchers who don't know the abbreviation; H4 addresses the convenience angle
- H2 and H5 rotate in Position 3 — H2 is problem-led (chips, the specific fear), H5 closes with authority and CTA

**Why H1 leads with the pipe:** Searchers who type "PPF Melbourne" are already past awareness — they know what PPF is. The pipe break makes the mobile differentiator unavoidable. "We Come To You" is the response to the implied objection ("but I'd have to drive somewhere").

### Descriptions (90 chars max)

| # | Description | Chars |
|---|-------------|-------|
| D1 | Mobile PPF in Toorak, Brighton & Bayside. Certified techs. No drop-off. Book online today. | 90 |
| D2 | Stone chips on the M1 aren't luck — they're physics. PPF stops them. We come to you. | 84 |

**Why D1 works:** Names three high-intent Melbourne suburbs before saying anything generic. "No drop-off" removes the primary logistical objection in eight characters. Closes with a CTA that is a statement, not a question.

**Why D2 works:** Reframes stone chips as a predictable, solvable physics problem, not random misfortune. "They're physics" shifts the reader from passive victim to someone who can act. The M1 is specifically the Eastern Freeway + Eastlink corridor — Toorak, Hawthorn, and South Yarra residents drive it daily. "We come to you" lands harder when placed after the problem description.

## Keyword Recommendations

### Exact Match — 5 Keywords

```
[paint protection film Melbourne]
[PPF installation Melbourne]
[mobile PPF Melbourne]
[PPF cost Melbourne]
[stone chip protection film Melbourne]
```

**Rationale:**
- `[paint protection film Melbourne]` — highest volume city-wide; catches buyers who know the full term
- `[PPF installation Melbourne]` — transactional intent; "installation" signals they're past research and ready to book
- `[mobile PPF Melbourne]` — directly matches our core differentiator; buyer is already pre-sold on mobile service
- `[PPF cost Melbourne]` — bottom-of-funnel comparison query; these users are price-checking before committing; our ad and landing page should address value, not just quote
- `[stone chip protection film Melbourne]` — problem-first search; buyer knows the problem but may not know "PPF" — captures that gap

---

### Negative Keywords — 3 to Exclude

```
[diy ppf]
[paint protection film kit]
[cheap ppf]
```

**Rationale:**
- `[diy ppf]` — research intent, not service intent; will burn budget on people who want to install it themselves
- `[paint protection film kit]` — product shoppers, not service buyers; the search signals they want to source film, not have it applied
- `[cheap ppf]` — price-anchors the wrong direction; Pristine's ICP pays for quality and expertise; this query attracts buyers who will baulk at professional PPF pricing
